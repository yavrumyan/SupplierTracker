* Purchase (Ստացված) invoice structure:
3rd row - headers. Data starts from 4th row.
Column B - Invoice number
Column D - Name of issuer (whom we bought from)
Column F - Date and time of issuance (date and time when we bought it)
Column I - Total Amount of that invoice
Column O - Type of invoice (Whether its an invoice for purchased goods or services)
Column R - Delivery address (Address, where the bought products were delivered)
Column U - Whether the invoice issuer works with VAT or not
Column AG - HS Code of the purchased product
Column AH - Name of the purchased product
Column AJ - Measurement unit used to measure total quantity of that product (units, meters, kilograms etc.)
Column AL - Quantity of the purchased product (how many pcs we purchased)
Column AM - Price without VAT of 1 pcs of purchased product
Column AO - Sum without VAT of all pcs of purchased product (Column AL*Column AM)
Column AS - VAT tax in percentage (usually 20%)
Column AT - Sum of VAT of all pcs of purchased product (Column AO*Column AS)
Column AV - Sum with VAT of all pcs of purchased product (Column AO+Column AT)

* Sales (Դուրս գրված) invoice structure:
3rd row - headers. Data starts from 4th row.
Column B - Invoice number
Column E - Name of invoice addressee (whom we sold)
Column G - Date and time of issuance (date and time when we bought it)
Column J - Total Amount of that invoice
Column P - Type of invoice (Whether its an invoice for purchased goods or services)
Column S - Delivery address (Address, where the bought products were delivered)
Column AI - HS Code of the purchased product
Column AJ - Name of the purchased product
Column AL - Measurement unit used to measure total quantity of that product (units, meters, kilograms etc.)
Column AN - Quantity of the purchased product (how many pcs we purchased)
Column AO - Price without VAT of 1 pcs of purchased product
Column AQ - Sum without VAT of all pcs of purchased product (Column AL*Column AM)
Column AU - VAT tax in percentage (usually 20%)
Column AV - Sum of VAT of all pcs of purchased product (Column AO*Column AS)
Column AX - Sum with VAT of all pcs of purchased product (Column AO+Column AT)
